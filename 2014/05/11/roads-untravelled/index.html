roads untravelled

By Jim rex

May 11 2014 Updated:May 12 2014

Weep not for roads untraveled

Weep not for paths left alone

’Cause beyond every bend

Is a long blinding end

It’s the worst kind of pain I’ve known

Today after 3 hours coding in the #E building,with just 2 problems accepted,I don’t feel quite well for my first time in ACM.

I had,honestly,never thought about taking part in this competition which included those who are very smart or expertized in algorithm.But this time I just want to travel the roads that I hadn’t coverd,I don’t try to disguse for my what I am not good at before;I should’t use “I didn’t take part in this game, so I don’have to be ashamed” as the excuse for my not doing well in this scope. And of course,I won’t be regretful for my missing some opportunities.If I am not appreciated, it just proves the fact that——NOW I AM NOT AS EXCELLENT AS I’VE EXPECTED.

I won’t lose my faith and loyalty to my heart,I don’t see the failure as a shame.It just showed me a sign that I should be better in the future than now.
